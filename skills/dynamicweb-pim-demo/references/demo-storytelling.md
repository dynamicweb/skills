# demo-storytelling.md

> PIM-flavoured demo tactics — governance and modelling-specific framing for a Dynamicweb 10 PIM demo. Generic narrative tactics that apply to any Dynamicweb demo (audience framing, multi-channel publishing tactics) live in `dynamicweb-demo-base/references/demo-tactics.md` — that split is deliberate; this file is intentionally PIM-narrow.

## PIM tactics

- **Hero SKU walkthrough** — pick ONE product and walk it end-to-end (draft → enriched → published across all channels) in under 15 min. Repeating the same SKU across tabs lets the audience track context.
- **Planted governance gaps are load-bearing** — intentionally leave 2–4 products missing a channel-critical field so the Completeness panel on those products turns red and the dashboard blocker-count widgets show non-zero. A demo where everything is green looks like theater; a demo where 2 products block a channel feels real.
- **Different products, different data models** — add at least two distinct product types (e.g. plants vs hardware, apparel vs accessories, or physical goods vs services) with their own data-model category. Shows taxonomy flexibility vs flat-schema competitors.
